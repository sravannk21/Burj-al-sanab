About Us:

Burj al sanable, a stainless steel distributing company, primarily focused on selling high quality stainless steel and CRA welded pipes. All of the pipes endorsed by us are Non-ferrous welded and rust-free. In order to ensure the premium quality of the products which we promote, we make sure that the manufacturers employ novel European-Japanese Machinery and Technology. We also make sure that our customers get timely assistance regarding any queries or grievances they have pertaining to the products.
